SIT223–SIT753 – Professional Practice in IT

Unit Details

Year: 2023 unit information
Enrolment modes: Trimester 1: Burwood, Geelong, Cloud
Credit point(s): 1
Unit Chair: Azadeh Ghari Neiat

Content

This unit introduces students to professional practice in IT, teamwork, communication skills, project planning and ethical responsibilities. Students will work in teams to develop real-world IT solutions and apply industry standards in project development.
